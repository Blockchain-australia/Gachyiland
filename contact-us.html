About Gachyi Land

Gachyi Land is an NFT Gacha-Launchpad. Every NFT launched on Gachyi Land’s Launchpad can either be built into its mini party games or Play2Earn MMORPG NFT game.

The name, Gachyi Land, comes from a few elements:

  • Element 1: Gachyi is a twist of the term Gachapon, which is the theme of our launchpad where all NFTs are minted via unique Gachapon machines.
  • Element 2: Chyi is the second first name of one of our founders.

Gachyi Land breathes by the rule where all NFTs serve a purpose throughout our ecosystem.

Gachyi Land’s own NFTs will either be built in its NFT games as playable characters or have in-game functionality such as boosts, extra drops or exclusive pre-sale access.

Other projects’ NFTs issued via the Gacha-Launchpad will also have some form of utility within the ecosystem depending on the commercial terms.
